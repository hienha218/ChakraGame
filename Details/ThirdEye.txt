Third-eye Chakra people always want to make change in the world.
They have unique life-style and have a need to break the norm.
They always looking for the truth and constantly want to bring justice into the world.
They also tend to have deep interest in hidden stuffs like occult, Tarot, etc.

The most common Sanskrit name for the Third Eye Chakra is Ajna
It is located on the forehead, between the eyebrows.
The function of the third eye chakra is driven by the principle of openness and imagination.
Most commonly represented with the color purple or bluish purple.
Physical associations: eyes, base of skull, brow
